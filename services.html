Our Services at OTH Insurance

At OTH Insurance, we offer a comprehensive range of insurance solutions designed to protect what matters most to you—whether it’s your home, your car, your health, your business, or your life. Our team is dedicated to providing personalized service, expert advice, and tailored policies that ensure you and your loved ones are fully covered. Explore the variety of services we offer, and let us help you find the right coverage for your needs.

1. Auto Insurance: Your car is more than just a mode of transportation—it’s an investment. OTH Insurance offers a range of auto insurance policies that cover everything from accidents and theft to natural disasters and vandalism. Whether you’re looking for basic coverage or a comprehensive plan, we have options that can be customized to fit your budget and driving habits.
Our Auto Insurance Coverage Includes:
- Collision Coverage.
- Comprehensive Coverage.
- Liability Insurance.
- Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Protection.
- Personal Injury Protection.
- Roadside Assistance.
We’ll work with you to create a policy that ensures peace of mind while on the road, knowing you have the protection you need when you need it most.

2. Home Insurance:
Your home is more than just a place to live; it’s a place where memories are made and cherished. Our home insurance policies offer protection for your property, belongings, and even personal liability. Whether you own or rent, we provide coverage that is designed to shield you from the unexpected, including damage from fires, floods, break-ins, and other unfortunate events.
Our Home Insurance Options Include:
- Property Protection (dwelling, structures, etc.).
- Personal Property Coverage (furniture, electronics, valuables).
- Liability Coverage.
- Loss of Use (temporary housing if your home is uninhabitable).
- Flood & Earthquake Insurance (optional).
- Renters Insurance (for tenants).
No matter your needs, OTH Insurance ensures that your home is fully protected, so you can focus on enjoying the moments that matter.

3. Life Insurance:
Planning for the future is a crucial step in ensuring the financial security of your loved ones. Our life insurance policies offer peace of mind, knowing that your family will be taken care of if something happens to you. We offer flexible life insurance options that can be customized to fit your goals, whether it’s providing for your children’s education or securing your spouse’s future.
Our Life Insurance Products Include:
- Term Life Insurance (affordable, fixed coverage for a set period).
- Whole Life Insurance (lifetime coverage with a savings component).
- Universal Life Insurance (flexible coverage with investment options).
- Final Expense Insurance (covering funeral and burial costs).
At OTH Insurance, we help you choose the right policy to ensure your loved ones are protected financially when the unexpected occurs.

Your health is your most valuable asset, and we’re here to help you protect it. OTH Insurance offers a variety of health insurance plans for individuals, families, and businesses. Whether you’re self-employed, between jobs, or simply looking for better coverage, we have a range of affordable options to keep you covered when you need it most.
Our Health Insurance Plans Include:

- Family Health Insurance.
- Group Health Insurance for Employers.
- Short-Term Health Insurance.

With OTH Insurance, you have access to reliable healthcare coverage that helps you manage costs and ensures you receive the care you deserve.
